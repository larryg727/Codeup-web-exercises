COACHELLA ACCOUNTS

A Coachella Account allows you to choose your lineup, activate your wristband, and post on the Coachella message board.

A Coachella.com Account is not your ticket purchase account and it will not recognize the login you used to purchase passes unless you happen to use the same log in for both websites. The websites are not related and do not talk to each other.

To log into your purchase account, please click on Weekend 1 or Weekend 2 to visit that website.
